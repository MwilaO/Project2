About me

My name is Mwila Otieno. I am an aspiring software engineer currently living
in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I was born overseas in Lusaka, Zambia to a mother
from the Bemba tribe, and a father from the Kenyan Luo tribe. My family
moved to the United States when I was two years old, settling in Pittsburgh.


For the majority of my schooling, due to financial hardships, my older brother and
I were homeschooled, which gave us both the opportunity to be around computers.
Because our parents both had backgrounds related to tech (Engineer and Computer
Scientist), we were heavily encouraged to learn how to progran. However, due to
stress from home life and untreated depression, it took me years of searching and
self reflection before I had the strength to pursue programming in earnest.


After several months of hard work and teaching myself the necessary code, I find
myself where I am today. Prepared to move forward into the world of software
engineering with confidence in myself and in my abilities.
